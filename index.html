NO STIGMA


Previous Next
No Stigma is a project I'm currently working on inspired by Humans of New York. It is a collection of stories from people who have lost loved ones to SUD(substance use disorder). Harris Insler lost his son to SUD and is working towards ending the stigma around SUD. The page will be a resource for people that want to understand life after loss to SUD and the ravaging effects it brings on the families and friends who remain to mourn and somehow go on. The page is expected to go live Spring 2020.
